# Successful Investors & Figures

This document compiles a list of expert investors and figures for the Audit & Correlation System.

## Target Figures

### Warren Buffett (Berkshire Hathaway)
- **Strategy:** Value investing, long-term focus, emphasis on competitive moats.
- **Key Events:** 1987 crash, 2008 financial crisis, various industry-specific shifts.

### Ray Dalio (Bridgewater Associates)
- **Strategy:** "Principles"-based macro investing, risk parity (All Weather portfolio).
- **Key Events:** Deleveragings, inflation cycles, global debt crises.

### Jim Simons (Renaissance Technologies)
- **Strategy:** Quantitative trading, mathematical models, pattern recognition.
- **Key Events:** Market volatility shifts, flash crashes, technological paradigm shifts.

### Cathie Wood (ARK Invest)
- **Strategy:** Growth investing, disruptive innovation, high-conviction thematic bets.
- **Key Events:** Technological breakthroughs (AI, genomics, blockchain), pandemic-driven shifts.

### George Soros (Quantum Fund)
- **Strategy:** Reflexivity, macro bets, contrarian approaches.
- **Key Events:** Breaking the Bank of England (1992), currency devaluations.

### Peter Lynch (Magellan Fund)
- **Strategy:** "Invest in what you know," growth at a reasonable price (GARP).
- **Key Events:** Bull market expansions, retail sector evolutions.

### Stanley Druckenmiller (Duquesne Capital)
- **Strategy:** Macro-driven, high-conviction, risk-focused.
- **Key Events:** Currency market shifts, interest rate changes.
